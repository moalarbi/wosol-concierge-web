# Wosol Concierge Website - Design Analysis

## Reference Analysis

### Royist.com Design Elements
- **Hero Section**: Dark background with dramatic mountain imagery, large circular geometric frame
- **Typography**: Elegant serif/script font for tagline "elevate your lifestyle™"
- **Color Scheme**: Dark navy/charcoal background with lime green accent borders on buttons
- **Layout**: Asymmetric with geometric shapes (circles, rectangles)
- **Services Grid**: 8 service cards in grid layout with clean typography
- **CTA Buttons**: Bordered style with lime green (#00FF00 or similar bright green)
- **Navigation**: Minimal top navigation with hamburger menu
- **Social Media**: Footer with social links

### Wosol Concierge Current Content
- Arabic language support (RTL)
- Services: Luxury events, health & wellness, luxury accommodations, luxury yachts
- Brand positioning: Luxury concierge for busy professionals
- Contact info: Riyadh-based, established 2011
- Newsletter subscription
- Social media: Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Snapchat, X-Twitter

### Wosol Strategy Hub Color Palette
- **Primary Green**: Bright lime green (#00FF00 or similar) - used for accents and borders
- **Background**: Light beige/cream (#F5F1E8 or similar)
- **Text**: Dark charcoal/black
- **Accent**: Muted beige/tan for secondary elements
- **Borders**: Dashed green lines for strategy cards

## Design Direction
- Combine Royist's luxury aesthetic with Wosol's Arabic/RTL support
- Use green accent color from Strategy Hub
- Dark luxury background with elegant typography
- Geometric design elements
- Arabic-first layout with RTL support
- Modern, premium feel suitable for luxury concierge service
